Pay As You Go

Our pricing model is designed to be flexible and cost-effective, allowing you to pay only for what you need as your business grows.

Flexible Pricing Options
With our growth-based pricing model, you can customize your plan to keep costs at a minimum. Here are the key factors that influence our pricing and help us offer you the best value:

  • Number of Stores:
    Adjust your plan based on the number of store locations you need to support.
  • Document Volume:
    Scale your plan according to the volume of documents processed.
  • Storage & Retention Period:
    Select the storage capacity and retention period that best fit your business requirements.
  • Contract Duration:
    Take advantage of pricing options based on the length of your contract.
  • Additional Integrations:
    Add only the integrations you need, avoiding unnecessary costs.
  • Third-Party Verifications:
    Include additional verification services as needed.
  • Support Plans:
    Choose the level of support that matches your operational needs.
  • Features Opted:
    Pick the specific features and functionalities that align with your business needs.
